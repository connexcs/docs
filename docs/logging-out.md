# Logging out of the ConnexCS Control Panel

---

**Document Metadata** <br>**Category**: ConnexCS Basics → Logging Out</br>
**Audience**: Administrators, Engineers, Support Team <br>**Difficulty**: Beginner</br>
**Time Required**: 1–2 minutes <br>**Prerequisites**: User has logged in to the ConnexCS control panel <br>**Related Topics**: [Changing Password](https://docs.connexcs.com/changing-password/), [Session Timeouts/Security](https://docs.connexcs.com/security/) <br>**Next Steps**: Confirm that your session is terminated—close your browser or clear your session cache for security.

---

When you no longer want to use the features on the portal, log out of the ConnexCS Control Panel.

To log out of the ConnexCS Control Panel:

1. Click the down-arrow next to your name (located at the top-right corner of the page).

    A task menu appears.

2. Click `Logout`.

    You are no longer logged in to the Control Panel.

    Your Web browser displays the Login Connex Carrier Services - Sign In pop-up.
