## Overview

**Next Generation 911 (NG911)** is an IP-based emergency communication framework designed to replace traditional circuit-switched emergency systems.

It enables enhanced capabilities such as multimedia communication (voice, text, video), improved location accuracy, and interoperability across networks.

NG911 is primarily relevant in regions such as the United States, where emergency services infrastructure is evolving to support modern, IP-based communication standards.

---

## ConnexCS Positioning

ConnexCS currently **does not provide full native support or compliance for NG911 standards**.

This includes, but is not limited to:

* End-to-end NG911 call routing architecture
* Native integration with Emergency Services IP Networks (ESInet)
* Full compliance with regulatory frameworks such as NENA i3

---

## Current Capabilities

While full NG911 compliance is not available, ConnexCS provides foundational capabilities that may support partial or indirect integration:

* SIP-based routing and interconnect flexibility
* Custom routing logic via platform configuration
* Integration with third-party carriers or emergency service providers
* Metadata handling within call detail records (CDRs)

These capabilities may be leveraged as part of a broader, partner-led NG911 implementation.

---

## Customer Requests & Custom Implementations

ConnexCS **welcomes requests from customers** who are exploring NG911-related use cases or require integration with NG911-compatible infrastructure.

Depending on the specific requirements, we can:

* Evaluate feasibility based on the target region and regulatory requirements
* Assist in designing routing workflows aligned with NG911 architectures
* Support integration with third-party NG911 service providers or compliant carriers
* Provide guidance on platform configuration to align with customer needs

---

### Important Considerations

Customers should be aware that:

* ConnexCS is **not a certified NG911 service provider**
* Compliance obligations remain with the customer and/or their upstream providers
* Implementation may require coordination with external vendors, carriers, and regulatory bodies
* Additional development or customization effort may be required

---

### Recommendation

Customers planning NG911 deployments are encouraged to:

* Engage with certified NG911 providers or ESInet operators
* Use ConnexCS as a flexible routing and integration layer where applicable
* Contact ConnexCS support or account management teams early in the planning phase
